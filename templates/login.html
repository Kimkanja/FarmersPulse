Data Privacy & Usage Policy

FarmersPulse Kenya · Last updated April 2025

1

What data we collect

We collect your name, email address, phone number, county, farm size, and crop types when you register. We may also collect usage patterns within the platform — such as pages visited and features used — to improve our services and tailor your experience.

2

How we use your data

  • Deliver personalised farming advisories based on your crops and location
  • Send weather alerts and forecasts relevant to your county
  • Provide market price updates for your specific crop types
  • Improve platform features and overall user experience
  • Send important service, account, and safety notifications
3

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share anonymised, aggregated data with agricultural research partners or government bodies — such as the Kenya Ministry of Agriculture — to support crop planning and national food security initiatives. Your individual identity is never disclosed.

4

Data security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.2+ in transit). Access is restricted to authorised FarmersPulse staff only. We conduct regular security audits and reviews to protect your information from unauthorised access.

5

Your rights

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data at any time
  • Withdraw consent at any time — your account will be deactivated
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) Kenya
  • Contact us at privacy@farmerspulse.co.ke for any data-related queries
6

Legal compliance

FarmersPulse operates in full compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019. By creating an account you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this policy. We reserve the right to update this policy — significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification.
